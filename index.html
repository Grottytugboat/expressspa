Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a spa delivery take in Melbourne?

Most Melbourne metro spa deliveries are completed within 1-2 business days. Regional Victoria and interstate deliveries vary based on distance and complexity. We'll provide a timeline during consultation.

Can you deliver directly from the factory?

Yes, we partner with many manufacturers across Australia for direct factory delivery. Contact us with your specifics and we'll arrange the pickup and delivery.

Is my spa insured during transport?

Absolutely. We're fully insured, giving you complete peace of mind throughout the relocation or delivery process. Your spa is covered from pickup to placement.

What if my backyard has tight access?

That's exactly what our light crane truck is designed for. We regularly deliver over fences, through narrow gates, and into tight backyards that standard trucks can't access.

How much does spa delivery cost in Melbourne?

Spa deliveries in Melbourne start from $500 depending on distance, access requirements, and spa size. Regional Victoria and interstate may vary. Contact us for an obligation-free quote.

What areas do you deliver spas to in Victoria?

We deliver across all of Victoria including Melbourne CBD, eastern and western suburbs, Geelong, Mornington Peninsula, Ballarat, Bendigo, Gippsland, and all regional areas. We also cover SA, NSW, and Southern QLD.
